Quick Info

Din Djarin

The Mandalorian in beskar armor, protector of Grogu and skilled warrior.

Universe:
Star Wars

Role:
Bounty Hunter

First Appearance:
The Mandalorian (November 2019)

Backstory

Din Djarin is a lone bounty hunter working in the Outer Reaches of the galaxy. After encountering Grogu, a Force-sensitive child, he takes on the responsibility of protecting the young one while seeking to find his people. His journey transforms him from a solitary mercenary to a dedicated protector and father figure.

Personality

Professional, stoic, and headstrong, Din hides his face behind his Mandalorian helmet. Beneath his stern exterior lies a compassionate warrior with strong moral convictions, particularly regarding the protection of children and the weak. He values the Mandalorian code and honor above all else.
